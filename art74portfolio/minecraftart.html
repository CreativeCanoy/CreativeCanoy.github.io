Minecraft Art

The purpose of this project was to create a sculpture of cultural significance. Star Wars has been around since the 1970's and for generations we have watched and grown to love the characters. In 2020 we were introduced to a new character "Grogu" from the same species as Yoda, giving hope to the future generations and Star Wars culture.
