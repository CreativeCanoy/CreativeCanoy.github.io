Self-Reflection Glitches

For my final project, I chose to glitch images of my reflection as a self-reflection in digital media art. These images represent my interests, hobbies, friends, where I grew up and myself as an artist. It does not matter if I'm admiring the ability of human creations or if I'm seeing a glimpse of how my friends see me, there is art in our reflections. Our reflection is just a reminder of who we are at that moment in time. Our reflections do not control us. Our reflections do not define us. We control our reflection.
