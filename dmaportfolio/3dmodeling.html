3D Modeling and Rendering

MAYA | MESHMIXER

Steven Canoy (2021) Food for Thought 1920 X 1080

For this piece I created a virtual reality. I wanted to explore textures and UV mapping images on polygonal models. Food for Thought is a surreal environment of giant foods that feed my guilty pleasures. As I get older I find myself thinking more about my choice of food; therefore, I created giant foods falling into a morphed hole with "The Thinker" looking as if he is contemplating the bad decisions.

MAYA | MESHMIXER

Steven Canoy (2021) Hologram Watch 960 x 540

In this piece I practiced 3D scanning with a app on my iPhone XR called "Trnio". I used my coworker as a model to scan. I wanted him to be a smooth textured statue in a public area. The background was a stretched image that gave a blur effect as if we took a photo focused on the subject giving him a realistic scene.

MINECRAFT

Steven Canoy (2021) Baby Yoda 1920 x 1200 (294 blocks)

This was my first time creating art in a 3D environment. Minecraft is similar to low resolution pixelated images. The art reminds me of Sega Genesis or Gameboy games and how their characters used to look. I created a head of Grogu aka "Baby Yoda" as my first piece because at the time, The Mandelorian season two just came out.
