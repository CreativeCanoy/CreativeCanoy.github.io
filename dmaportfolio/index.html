About

Myself

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Steven Canoy and I am a student at SJSU. Growing up in the Silicon Valley inspired me to educate myself in Digital Media Art. Everything can be turned into art especially in the digital world. I enjoy seeing the possibilities of coding when the code is properly functioning. Also, working with RAW data and bending it to create a glitch in the system can be chaotically beautiful. When it comes to creating art, I take a very literal approach. It may be my biggest challenge when breaking out of the litteral mindset and stepping into the creative side. I want my art to be pleasing and colorful or artisticly dark. Welcome to my artisic side.
