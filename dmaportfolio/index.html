About

Myself

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Steven Canoy and I am a student at SJSU. Growing up in the Silicon Valley inspired me to educate myself in Digital Media Art. Everything can be turned into art from web designing to javascript. I enjoy seeing the possibilities of coding when the code is properly functioning, although reppetition of finding my errors helps me spot the issues sooner. Also, working with RAW data and bending it to create a glitch in the system can be chaotically beautiful. When it comes to creating art, I take a very literal approach. It may be my biggest challenge when breaking out of the litteral mindset and stepping into the creative side. I want my art to be colorful or beautifully dark. Welcome to my artisic side.
