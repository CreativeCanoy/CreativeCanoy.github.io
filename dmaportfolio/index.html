About

Myself

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Steven Canoy and I recently graduated from SJSU with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Media Art. Growing up in the Silicon Valley inspired me to educate myself in Digital Media Art because I am surrounded by giant tech companies like Apple, Facebook, Google, Tesla, etc. I grew up drawing and DJing as hobbies so I always felt like I was attracted to art. When it comes to Design, my brain is pulled in different directions between minimalist styles and exotic features. I like to create ways of playing with perception and something viewers can engage with.
