The location I chose was known as 4th St. Pizza Co. and it was a great pizza parlor / bar for families and friends. The 16 year old business closed its doors after their lease expired in October of 2022. Bayview Development Company purchased the building before the pandemic and plans to demolish the building to replace it with a muli-story structure.
