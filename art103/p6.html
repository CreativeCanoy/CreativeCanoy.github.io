My Answers
  • Q1. We portrayed dinosaurs as giant lizards, hairless, muscular and slow, but now they have change to describe dinosaurs as having bird feathers, athletic, different skins or possibly even chunky.
  • Q2. . I would guess Nasa and the scientists who determine how other planets look like. We have information such as what chemicals make up a planet and images of earth mars and the moon so it helps us interpret what the surface of a planet might look like. Artists create possible renders of planets that we haven’t been on. Another example would be Crime, and finding a suspect. Details about their appearance or clothes will help an artist create a sketch of a possible suspect.
  • Q3. . Material speculation to me right now means: When something is made to seem as if it is from a certain place, time, or world and subliminally implying as if it is, when it is not.
