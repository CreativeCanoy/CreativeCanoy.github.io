In this phase, we created tessellations on Adobe Illustrator and came up with our own design along with a common side that we all agreed on. The First image shows our individual designs and the second image shows variations of how they can tessellate.

Phase 2

For this design, I used plike board and I tried to 'hand cut' throughole finger tenons. The design was really rough because I was not able to get those little detailed cuts like a laser cutter, but the exercise helped me understand how it works.
