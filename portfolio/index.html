About

Myself

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Steven Canoy and I am a graduate from San Jose State University with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Media Art. I grew up playing drawing, playing sports, and DJing as hobbies so I always felt attracted to any form of sart. Being raised in the Silicon Valley inspired me to educate myself in D.M.A. because we are surrounded by giant tech companies with products I use all the time. I love to play video games and have created a few of my own with a small team, but when it comes to Design, my brain is pulled in different directions between minimalist styles and exotic features. I enjoy designing all kinds of art wether it is to be clean and simple or exotic and complex. I like to create ways of playing with perception and something viewers can engage with.
