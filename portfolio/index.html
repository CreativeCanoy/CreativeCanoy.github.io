About

Myself

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Steven Canoy, a graduate of San Jose State University, where I earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Media Art. Growing up, I had a passion for drawing, sports, and DJing, which naturally drew me to various forms of art. Living in the Silicon Valley, surrounded by tech giants and their innovative products, inspired me to explore the world of D.M.A. I'm an avid gamer and have even had the opportunity to develop a few small games of my own. However, when it comes to design, I find myself torn between minimalist styles and incorporating exotic features. I thoroughly enjoy designing art that ranges from clean and simple to intricate and elaborate. I love to create artwork that plays with perception and engages viewers on a deeper level.
